Volunteer Opportunities

We have various ways you can contribute your time and skills

Teaching

Help children with their studies and conduct fun learning activities.

  • 2-4 hours per week
  • Subject expertise preferred
  • Training provided

Child Care

Spend quality time with children and organize recreational activities.

  • Flexible hours
  • Great for students

Mentoring

Guide older children with career advice and life skills.

  • Monthly meetings
  • Professional experience helpful

Professional Skills

Offer your professional skills (IT, design, marketing, etc.).

  • Project-based
  • Remote options available
  • Great for corporate teams
