About Sukoon

Our journey, mission, and the team behind our work

Our Story

Sukoon was founded in 2025 by a group of passionate individuals who recognized the growing need for child welfare services in underserved communities. What began as a small idea to provide basic education to street children has now grown into a foundation.

Over the past decade, we've impacted the lives of over 50,000 children through our various programs and initiatives. Our name 'Sukoon', meaning 'peace' and 'tranquility', reflects our commitment to providing children with the safety and stability they deserve.

Our Mission

To provide every child with access to education, healthcare, and a safe environment where they can grow and thrive, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Vision

A world where every child has the opportunity to realize their full potential and lead a life of dignity and purpose.
